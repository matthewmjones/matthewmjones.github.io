Facts

I earned my PhD in Pure Mathematics from UCL in 1999 and have accumulated over twenty years of experience in academia. Throughout my career, I've held numerous senior academic management roles, overseeing significant changes in large and intricate educational programs. I have a proven track record of designing and developing successful programs, as well as leading sizable teams. My primary teaching focus revolves around mathematics and computing modules. Additionally, I have authored several articles spanning mathematics education practice and pure mathematics research. My research aims to understand the processes through which students acquire and assimilate mathematical knowledge, with a particular emphasis on fostering rational problem-solving approaches.
