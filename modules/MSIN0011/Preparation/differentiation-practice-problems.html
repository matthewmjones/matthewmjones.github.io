Topic 2 Differentiation Practice Problems

2.1 Problems

Differentiate the following with respect to \(x\), simplifying your answers where possible.

  1. \(x(2x+5)^7\)
  2. \(x^2(2x+5)^7\)
  3. \(x^4(x+3)^5\)
  4. \(x^4(x^2+3)^5\)
  5. \(x^3(3-x^4)^5\)
  6. \((x^2-7)(x^4+1)^2\)
  7. \((2x^2+1)^3(3x-1)^7\)
  8. \((3x+5)^9(2x-1)^6\)
  9. \((4-x)^5(2x+1)^3\)
  10. \(x\sqrt{x+3}\)
  11. \(x^2\sqrt{x+3}\)
  12. \((x+3)^4\sqrt{x}\)
  13. \((x+3)^2\sqrt{2x^2+1}\)
  14. \(\displaystyle \frac{2x}{x+3}\)
  15. \(\displaystyle \frac{3x}{x-5}\)
  16. \(\displaystyle \frac{3x-1}{2x+3}\)
  17. \(\displaystyle \frac{1-5x}{3x+2}\)
  18. \(\displaystyle \frac{2x}{x^2+4}\)
  19. \(\displaystyle \frac{1-x}{x^2+3}\)
  20. \(\displaystyle \frac{3-x}{x^2+16}\)
  21. \(\displaystyle \frac{x^5}{(x+1)^3}\)
  22. \(\displaystyle \frac{3x}{(x-2)^4}\)
  23. \(\displaystyle \frac{(6x+5)^2}{(2x-3)^3}\)
  24. \(\displaystyle \frac{4x+3}{\sqrt{2x-1}}\)
  25. \(\sin 2x\)
  26. \(\sec 5x\)
  27. \(\csc 6x\)
  28. \(\cos (x^2 + 1)\)
  29. \(\sin (2x^2+1)\)
  30. \(\sec (4x^2 + 1)\)
  31. \(\cot (2x^2 + 5)\)
  32. \(\sin^2 x\)
  33. \(\cos^3 x\)
  34. \(\tan^4 x\)
  35. \(\sec^6 x\)
  36. \(\tan^3 5x\)
  37. \(\sin^3 x \cos 3x\)
  38. \(\cos^4 x \sin 4x\)
  39. \(\displaystyle \frac{\cos^4 x}{x^2}\)
  40. \(\displaystyle \frac{\cos^3 5x}{6x}\)
  41. \(e^{2x}\)
  42. \(5 e^{2x^3}\)
  43. \(3x^3 e^{2x}\)
  44. \(\displaystyle \frac{3x^2}{e^x}\)
  45. \(\ln 2x^3\)
  46. \(\ln \sin x\)
  47. \(\ln 3^x\)
  48. \(x\ln x\)
